Feedback

By now, I've invested a fair amount of time in ProGuard. You can help by providing feedback! If you have good experiences, bad experiences, problems, bugs, bug fixes, ideas, encouragements, etc. to share, please go ahead:

  • If you need help with ProGuard, please go to the help forum (at SourceForge).

  • A good place to share your thoughts is the open discussion forum (at SourceForge).

  • You can submit and consult bug reports on the bug tracking page (at SourceForge).

  • Similarly, you can submit and consult feature requests on the feature request page (at SourceForge).

  • If you just want to stay abreast of the latest developments, you can subscribe to the announcements of new releases in the download section at SourceForge, or the project page at FreshMeat.

  • For anything that doesn't fall in the above categories, you can mail me directly at .

I can't promise a swift answer, or any answer at all, for that matter, but I like seeing any constructive comments.

ProGuard isn't a typical open source project, in the sense that I am not looking for code contributions. Developing on my own allows me to do things my way, without the overhead of project management and the compromises associated with larger projects.
